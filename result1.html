You're a Potato!

Arguably the most versatile of vegetables. They are the source of chips, mashed potatoes, curly fries, tater tots, hash browns, potato waffles, croquettes... The possibilities are endless! And how could we forget, vodka comes from potatoes - incredible! You can even use potatoes to make electricity.

So, as the potato of your friend group, people often mistake you as the lazy one, but actually you are loved by all, you're adaptable, you've got many skills, and you're all-round a pretty cool person. However, you don't count as one of your 5 a day, sorry... Take that as you will.
